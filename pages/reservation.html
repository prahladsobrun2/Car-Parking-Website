Reservation Guidelines

  • Before reserving, make sure:
    • Your vehicle number is correct
    • You select an available parking slot
    • You arrive within your selected time
  • After reservation:
    • Follow on-site parking signs
    • Park only in your reserved slot
    • Contact staff if assistance is needed
